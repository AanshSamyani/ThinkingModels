To determine the probability of drawing a blue marble from the bag, follow these steps:

1. **Calculate the total number of marbles in the bag:**
   \[
   \text{Total marbles} = \text{Red marbles} + \text{Blue marbles} + \text{Green marbles} = 10 + 8 + 6 = 24
   \]

2. **Determine the number of favorable outcomes (drawing a blue marble):**
   \[
   \text{Blue marbles} = 8
   \]

3. **Compute the probability:**
   \[
   \text{Probability of drawing a blue marble} = \frac{\text{Number of blue marbles}}{\text{Total number of marbles}} = \frac{8}{24} = \frac{1}{3}
   \]
